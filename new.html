About US

Welcome to SM Mess, where we bring you the authentic flavors of Kerala. Located in the serene town of Padur, Alathur, our mess is known for its delicious and traditional Kerala cuisine. At SM Mess, we believe in preserving the rich culinary heritage of Kerala and serving it with love and care. Our experienced chefs prepare each dish with fresh ingredients and aromatic spices, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience. From the famous Appam with Stew to the mouthwatering Malabar Fish Curry, our menu offers a wide range of authentic Kerala delicacies. We carefully select the finest local ingredients to bring out the true essence of Kerala's vibrant flavors. Our warm and welcoming ambiance, along with our attentive staff, creates a delightful atmosphere for you to savor the exquisite taste of Kerala. Whether you're a local resident or a visitor to Padur, we invite you to immerse yourself in the culinary delights of Kerala at SM Mess. Experience the traditional taste of Kerala cuisine, handed down through generations, and indulge in the unique blend of spices and flavors that make our dishes truly special. We take pride in sharing the rich heritage of Kerala's culinary traditions with every customer we serve. Come and join us at SM Mess for an authentic dining experience that will transport you to the enchanting land of Kerala.
